Istanbul Jazz Festival: Night of Smooth Jazz

Date and Time: Friday, May 10th, 2024, 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Location: Harbiye Cemil Topuzlu Open-Air Theater, Istanbul

The Istanbul Jazz Festival is an annual celebration of jazz music, bringing together talented musicians from around the world to enchant audiences with their soulful melodies and captivating performances. Held in the heart of Istanbul, this festival is a testament to the city's vibrant cultural scene and its deep appreciation for the art of jazz. From smooth improvisations to lively ensembles, the festival offers a diverse lineup of performances that cater to jazz enthusiasts of all tastes. Whether you're a seasoned jazz aficionado or simply looking to immerse yourself in the magic of live music, the Istanbul Jazz Festival promises an unforgettable experience filled with rhythm, harmony, and endless inspiration. Join us as we celebrate the timeless allure of jazz and create unforgettable memories under the starlit skies of Istanbul.
